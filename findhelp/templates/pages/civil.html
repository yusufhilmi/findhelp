{% trans 'How our platform works?' %}

{% blocktrans %} Our users are able to create help calls on the system. Whilst creating it, they are providing us a brief description of their situation, their contact information and the relevant category for them.
Categories are decided by our team considering our partnerships and our potential effect on that category.

After help calls we are connecting our users with a relevant civil organization.

We are gradually automating the worflow to provide email notifications at instant. With Natural Language Processing we hope to create a total automation on validating help calls and finding the most relevant civil organizations.

{% endblocktrans %}
